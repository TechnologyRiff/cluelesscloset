About Clueless Closet

Clueless Closet is an app that shows and organizes your virtual closet. It relieves the stress of everyday fashion dilemmas for people short on time who need a quick and convenient solution for choosing what to wear. Clueless Closet reduces the amount of time it takes to get ready in the morning by arranging an outfit based on weather, events, and color scheme. After using this quick and easy app, you will feel confident, organized, and on top of your game, ready to take on your day!

Features

  • uploading photos
  • weather
  • calendar
  • clothing categories
  • personalized profile
